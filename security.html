Data & Infrastructure

Where your data lives and how it's protected.

Hosting & data residency

All customer data is hosted in the European Union. Infrastructure runs on ISO 27001-certified cloud providers with data centers in the EU.

🔐

Encryption

Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Encryption keys are managed per customer. Backups are encrypted with the same standards.

🧱

Tenant isolation

Every customer environment is logically isolated. Your documents, training content, and user data are never accessible to other customers.
