Every training output is cited to your source documents — SOPs, incident reports, regulatory standards. If it's not in the source, it's not in the training. AI generates the format, never the safety content.

Your documents never train our models. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Isolated per customer. Delete on request — no retention, no exceptions.

Human sign-off on every module

AI drafts. Humans approve. Every training module passes through your HSE team before a single worker sees it. No auto-publish, no shortcuts.
