Confined Space Entry

Confined space incidents are among the deadliest in industrial workplaces — often claiming multiple lives in a single event. This module covers the hazards, procedures, and emergency protocols for working in confined spaces, built from real incident data and adapted to your site-specific permit-to-work systems.

Who this is for

🔧

Entrants & Operators

Workers who physically enter confined spaces — tanks, vessels, manholes, silos, or pipelines — to perform maintenance, inspection, or cleaning tasks.

👁️

Attendants & Hole Watch

Personnel stationed outside the confined space to monitor conditions, maintain communication, and initiate emergency response if needed.

📋

Supervisors & Permit Issuers

Team leads responsible for issuing confined space permits, verifying atmospheric conditions, and authorizing entry and rescue plans.

🚨

Rescue Teams

On-site emergency response personnel who need to execute confined space rescue without becoming secondary casualties themselves.

Oil & Gas Chemical & Petrochemical Water & Wastewater Manufacturing Mining Construction

What this module covers

Confined space identification & classification
Permit-to-work procedures
Entry & exit protocols
PPE selection for confined spaces
Atmospheric testing & gas detection
Ventilation requirements & monitoring
Rescue planning & emergency response
Communication systems & attendant duties

Learning objectives

01 Identify confined spaces and assess the risks before entry, including atmospheric hazards, engulfment, and entrapment.
02 Execute a complete permit-to-work process from hazard assessment through to space sign-off, including atmospheric testing and ventilation checks.
03 Respond to confined space emergencies following rescue protocols without becoming a secondary casualty.

Frequently asked questions

Does this replace in-person confined space training?
It depends on your regulatory context. In most jurisdictions, Akdo modules cover the theory and procedural knowledge required for confined space entry, while practical assessments (equipment handling, rescue drills) still happen on site. We help you map our content to your local regulations so you know exactly what's covered.
How is the content customized to our company?
We use your actual incident reports, near-miss data, site photos, and standard operating procedures to build the training. Your workers see their own confined spaces, their own permit systems, and their own gas detection equipment — not generic stock content.
What happens when we have a new confined space incident?
Share the investigation report and we generate new training content within days — a safety alert video, updated microlearning lessons, or a new simulation scenario. No waiting months for a vendor to revise a PowerPoint.
Can workers access this on mobile or offline?
Yes. Microlearning lessons are delivered through Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or your LMS — wherever your workforce already is. Video content works on any device. For remote sites with limited connectivity, modules can be pre-loaded for offline access.
How do we track who has completed the training?
Akdo provides real-time dashboards showing completion rates, comprehension scores, and knowledge gaps across teams, sites, and individuals. We integrate with your existing LMS via SCORM/xAPI, or you can use our built-in tracking if you prefer.
Which regulations does this cover?
The module is aligned to OSHA 1910.146 (US), the Confined Spaces Regulations 2014 (UK), EN 547 (EU), and IOGP Life-Saving Rules. We adapt the content to your specific regulatory requirements — whether that's local legislation, client standards, or internal policies.
