Confined Space Entry

A comprehensive training module covering the hazards, procedures, and emergency protocols for working in confined spaces. Built from real incident data and adapted to your site-specific permit-to-work systems.

What this module covers

Confined space identification & classification
Atmospheric testing & gas detection
Permit-to-work procedures
Ventilation requirements & monitoring
Entry & exit protocols
Rescue planning & emergency response
PPE selection for confined spaces
Communication systems & attendant duties

Learning objectives

01 Identify confined spaces and assess the risks before entry, including atmospheric hazards, engulfment, and entrapment.
02 Execute a complete permit-to-work process from hazard assessment through to space sign-off.
03 Perform atmospheric testing using gas detection equipment and interpret results against safe entry thresholds.
04 Respond to confined space emergencies following rescue protocols without becoming a secondary casualty.
