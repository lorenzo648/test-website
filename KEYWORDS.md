## Keywords We're NOT Targeting

| Keyword | Why |
|---------|-----|
| `VR safety training` / `virtual reality training` | No VR features |
| `safety management software` | Inspections/audits — not training |
| `safety inspection app` | SafetyCulture territory |
| `EHS management platform` | Broader suite — not Akdo's scope |
| `LMS` / `learning management system` | Akdo integrates with LMS, isn't one |
| `safety culture platform` | Too vague, Pixaera's territory |
