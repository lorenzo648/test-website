HSE Safety

Working at Heights

Falls from height remain the leading cause of workplace fatalities globally. This module trains workers to recognize fall hazards, inspect equipment, and follow safe work procedures — built from real incident data and adapted to your specific sites, equipment, and permit systems.

Who this is for

Frontline Workers

Operators, technicians, and trades workers who perform tasks at elevation — scaffolding, roofwork, tower access, or platform operations.

Supervisors & Foremen

Team leads responsible for work permits, pre-task briefings, and ensuring their crew follows height safety protocols on site.

HSE Managers

Safety professionals who need to deploy, track, and verify height safety training across multiple sites and contractor teams.

Contractors

Third-party teams working on your sites who need to meet your specific height safety standards before mobilization.

What this module covers

Fall hazard identification & risk assessment
Harness inspection & correct fitting
Scaffold safety & edge protection
Ladder safety & 3-point contact
Anchor point selection & load ratings
Permit-to-work for height activities
Dropped object prevention
Rescue planning & suspension trauma

Learning objectives

01 Identify fall hazards and select the correct prevention or protection system for the task — guardrails, restraint, or fall arrest.
02 Inspect a full-body harness and lanyard before use, identifying wear, damage, and expiration that require removal from service.
03 Execute an emergency rescue plan for a suspended worker, recognizing the signs of suspension trauma and time-critical interventions.

Frequently asked questions

Does this replace classroom-based height safety training?
It depends on your regulatory context. In many jurisdictions, Akdo modules cover the theory component of working at heights certification, while practical assessments still happen on site. We help you map our content to your local regulations so you know exactly what's covered and what still requires in-person delivery.
How is the content customized to our company?
We use your actual incident reports, near-miss data, site photos, and standard operating procedures to build the training. Your workers see their own workplace, their own equipment, and their own permit systems — not generic stock content. Your branding and terminology are used throughout.
What happens when we have a new height-related incident?
Share the investigation report and we generate new training content within days — a safety alert video, updated microlearning lessons, or a new simulation scenario. No waiting months for a vendor to revise a PowerPoint.
Can workers access this on mobile or offline?
Yes. Microlearning lessons are delivered through Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or your LMS — wherever your workforce already is. Video content works on any device. For remote sites with limited connectivity, modules can be pre-loaded for offline access.
How do we track who has completed the training?
Akdo provides real-time dashboards showing completion rates, comprehension scores, and knowledge gaps across teams, sites, and individuals. We integrate with your existing LMS via SCORM/xAPI, or you can use our built-in tracking if you prefer.
Which regulations does this cover?
The module is aligned to OSHA 1926 Subpart M (US), the Work at Height Regulations 2005 (UK), EN 363 (EU), and IOGP Life-Saving Rules. We adapt the content to your specific regulatory requirements — whether that's local legislation, client standards, or internal policies.
