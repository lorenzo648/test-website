5 — How it works

How it works

From a 30-minute call to live training. Four steps.

1. Discovery call

30-minute call. We learn your industry, your sites, your hazards. You share SOPs and incident reports.

⏱ 30 min

2. Akdo builds

AI generates videos, scenarios, and assessments from your documents. Every output linked back to your documents.

⚡ Days, not months

3. You review & approve

Your HSE team reviews every module. Edit, adjust, request changes. Nothing goes live without your sign-off.

🛡 Human in the loop

4. Deploy everywhere

Export as SCORM to your existing LMS, or let Akdo host it for you. Your team picks the setup, we handle the rest.

🌐 Plug and play

Go live in 12 weeks. Less than 20 hours of your team's time.
