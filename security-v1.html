Data & Infrastructure

Where your data lives and how it's protected.

Hosting & data residency

All customer data is hosted in the European Union. Infrastructure runs on ISO 27001-certified cloud providers with data centers in the EU. No customer data leaves the region.

Encryption

Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Encryption keys are managed per customer. Backups are encrypted with the same standards.

Tenant isolation

Every customer environment is logically isolated. Your documents, training content, and user data are never accessible to other customers — in storage, processing, or AI inference.

Data ownership & deletion

You own your data. Your documents never train our models. On contract termination, all data is deleted within 30 days — no retention, no exceptions. Data export available on request.
